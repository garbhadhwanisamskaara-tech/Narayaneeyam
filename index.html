Sriman Narayaneeyam — Your Gateway to Divine Grace

A sacred journey through 100 Dashakams — chant, learn, and grow in devotion with the divine grace of Guruvayurappan. Composed by Melpathur Narayana Bhattathiri in 1586 CE at the Guruvayur temple, Sriman Narayaneeyam condenses the Bhagavata Purana into 1,034 luminous Sanskrit verses.

What is Narayaneeyam?

Narayaneeyam is a 16th-century devotional Sanskrit work in 100 Dashakams (groups of ten verses) narrating the leelas of Lord Krishna. It is chanted daily by thousands of devotees worldwide for health, peace and divine grace.
